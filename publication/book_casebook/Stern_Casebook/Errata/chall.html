Errata for the case

The flight of the space shuttle Challenger

p. 33:

The launch date for the Challenger was January 28, 1986, not January 20, 1986.

p. 34:

As is pointed out in Chapter 2 of the book Visual Explanations: Images and Quantities, Evidence and Narrative, by Edward R. Tufte, the table and scatter plot were not actually constructed on the night of January 27, 1986, but were rather given later by two Morton Thiokol staff members as an example of the reasoning used in the pre-launch debate. The actual charts faxed from Morton Thiokol to NASA that night were considerably less informative than even these seriously flawed presentations.
